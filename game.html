Lottery - Like Betting Game

In Red Keno, there are 80 balls numbered from 1 to 80 loaded into the lotto machine and during the draw, 20 balls are selected at random. The player can choose from 1 to 8 numbers and his prize depends on how many numbers selected by him are the same as those drawn. The more matches, the greater his gain. Furthermore, with Red Keno you have even more chances to win thanks to two bonus balls!

Characteristics of Red Keno

Bonus balls

Once in a while, there are two additional bonus balls being drawn which increases players' chances of winning.

Jackpots

Once in a while, there are two additional bonus balls being drawn which increases players' chances of winning.

Pay tables

Several unique pay tables with the possibility to configure one to your preferences.

Types of bets

Exacta

The player bets that the selected participant finishes first.

In First 3

The player bets that the selected participant finishes first, second or third.

Perfecta

The player bets that the selected two participants finish first and second in the exact order.

Over/Under

The player bets that the winner of the race will be the participant from the first starting three (Under) or from the second starting three (Over).

Quinella

The player bets that the selected two participants finish first and second, but not necessary in the exact order.

Even/Odd

The player bets that the winner of the race will be the participant under Even or Odd number

Trifecta

The player bets that the selected three participants finish first, second and third in the exact order
